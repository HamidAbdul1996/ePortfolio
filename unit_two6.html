Research Questions, the Literature Review and the Research Proposal

Initial thoughts

Unit 2 served as a valuable refresher on research questions, literature reviews, and research proposals. While I have conducted literature reviews before, the reminder was beneficial. My memory of research proposals was hazy, so this unit was particularly helpful. I've begun exploring the literature on misinformation detection using machine learning and am starting to brainstorm potential research proposals. My goal is to identify an unresearched aspect within this field. As highlighted in the unit, a well-defined research question is crucial as it provides a clear focus and direction for the project, defining the scope, guiding literature selection, and determining the appropriate methodology. Ultimately, a strong research question ensures that the findings are both relevant and meaningful to the field of study (Fandino, 2019).

Literature Review Proposal Outline

This research proposes to investigate the effectiveness of machine learning (ML) in detecting misinformation/disinformation on social media. Given the escalating threat of misinformation to societal trust, exacerbated by the rise of generative AI, this study aims to develop a robust, real-time detection model. The research will build upon existing literature, addressing gaps in cross-lingual and cross-cultural detection, feature identification, and model explainability. The research will potentially employ advanced ML/DL techniques, such as LSTM, CNN, and BERT, and explore the integration of explainable AI methods to enhance transparency. The expected outcomes include a refined detection model, insights into misinformation dissemination across languages and cultures, and contributions to the field of explainable AI. Ultimately, this research seeks to empower individuals and institutions to better identify and counter political misinformation, safeguarding democratic discourse in the digital age.

Reflection

This unit has helped me develop ideas for my literature review proposal and my final research proposal. I have picked misinformation detection on a social media website, I am leaning towards X(Twitter) as there is more data available for this social media website. This is a project that is of great importance and also something I find interesting. One concern I have is around the project’s feasibility. I’m always kind of unsure if it’s feasible. I will start early so I have ample time to reassess and adopt if challenges arise.
