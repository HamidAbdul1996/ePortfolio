Unit 10: Research Writing

Initial thoughts

This unit proved highly beneficial, especially as I plan to transition from my current public sector role into the tech industry. The emphasis on effectively communicating technical knowledge is particularly relevant to my career goals. Additionally, the insights into writing technical dissertations were valuable, as my undergraduate background in Politics didn't cover this skillset. While I hadn't previously considered publishing research, the information provided was intriguing. If, in my data science career, I conduct high-quality research, I may now pursue publication as a potential avenue. Strong research writing skills demonstrate an ability to think critically, communicate effectively and contribute to the field which can only help in my career advancement. (University of Derby, 2019).
